# 🤝 Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

### 🎯 Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to:

- **Be respectful and inclusive**: Treat everyone with respect and kindness
- **Be helpful and supportive**: Help others learn and grow
- **Be constructive**: Provide constructive feedback and suggestions
- **Be professional**: Maintain professional communication and behavior
- **Be collaborative**: Work together to achieve common goals
- **Be patient**: Remember that everyone has different learning speeds
- **Be open-minded**: Be willing to learn from others and consider different perspectives

### 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

- **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, including sexual harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- **Discrimination**: Discrimination based on age, gender, race, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or any other characteristic
- **Personal attacks**: Insults, ad hominem attacks, or personal criticism
- **Spam**: Unwanted commercial content, self-promotion, or off-topic content
- **Trolling**: Deliberate provocation or disruption
- **Threats**: Threats of violence or harm
- **Privacy violations**: Sharing personal information without consent
- **Intellectual property violations**: Plagiarism, copyright infringement, or other IP violations

## 📚 Specific Guidelines for This Project

### 📖 Documentation Contributions
- Ensure content is accurate and up-to-date
- Use inclusive language and examples
- Provide clear explanations for complex concepts
- Include diverse examples that appeal to different backgrounds

### 💻 Code Contributions
- Follow Ruby style guidelines
- Write clear, well-documented code
- Include tests when appropriate
- Ensure code is accessible to different skill levels

### 🤝 Community Interaction
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Answer questions patiently and thoroughly
- Share knowledge and experiences
- Encourage diverse perspectives and approaches

## 🛡️ Enforcement

### Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please:

1. **Contact the maintainers**:
   - Email: [maintainer-email@example.com]
   - GitHub: [Create a private issue](https://github.com/SENODROOM/Ruby/issues/new)
   - Discord: [Join our community server](https://discord.gg/ruby-guide)

2. **Provide details**:
   - What happened
   - When and where it happened
   - Who was involved
   - Any evidence (screenshots, logs, etc.)

3. **Be specific**:
   - Include as much detail as possible
   - Provide context if helpful
   - Suggest resolution if appropriate

### Resolution Process

1. **Initial Response**: We will respond within 48 hours
2. **Investigation**: We will investigate the report thoroughly
3. **Action**: We will take appropriate action based on the severity
4. **Follow-up**: We will follow up with the reporter and involved parties

### Consequences

Depending on the severity and nature of the violation, consequences may include:

- **Warning**: For minor violations or first-time offenses
- **Temporary Suspension**: For repeated or more serious violations
- **Permanent Ban**: For severe violations or repeated offenses after warnings
- **Content Removal**: For inappropriate content in documentation or examples
- **Project Removal**: For contributors who repeatedly violate the code of conduct
